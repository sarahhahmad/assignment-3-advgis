New York City Community Land Trusts

Community Land Trusts (CLT) are becoming an increasingly valuable tool for communities in New York to participate in community-centered land acquisition. CLTs are a way for communities to prevent displacement and ensure long-term housing affordability.

This map locates where current CLTs are in New York City and their land acquisition status. This map builds upon Pratt Center for Community Development's static map by adding interactive components.
